1. Game Overview

Hide & Seek is a continuous game of cat-and-mouse played across a designated local transit system. One player (the Hider) uses public transit to travel to a secret location and establish a Hiding Zone (more on this below). The remaining players (the Seekers) work cooperatively to track down the Hider by asking strategic questions.

The game operates in rounds. Once the Seekers can visually see the Hider, the round ends, and a new player becomes the Hider. The player who remains hidden for the longest total time wins the game.

2. Setting Up the Game

  • The Map: The game map is strictly defined by the \"Calgary Rapid Transit Network\". All gameplay and movement must occur within the bounds of this network.
  • The Head Start: Before the Seekers can begin their pursuit, the Hider is granted a designated head start period (e.g., 45 to 60 minutes, depending on the agreed-upon game size).
  • Transit Only: During the head start, the Hider may only travel using approved public transit (trains, buses) and walking. No private vehicles or ride-shares are allowed.

3. The Hiding Zone

Once the head start concludes, the Hider must permanently lock in their final Hiding Zone.

  • The Anchor Station: The Hider must choose a valid transit station to act as the anchor for their location.
  • Zone Radius: The Hider must remain within a specific radius of their Anchor Station for the remainder of the round.
  • Stationary Rule: Once the Hiding Zone is locked in, the Hider cannot leave this radius until they are caught.
  • Visuallizing the Hiding Zones: Use the right sidebar (pin icon) to toggle on/off the visuals for the Hiding Zones.

4. The Seeking Phase & Questions

To locate the Hider, Seekers use the webapp to ask specific types of questions. The Hider must answer truthfully.

Question Categories

matching

Seekers ask if the Hider 's location shares a specific attrtibute with them (e.g., 'Does your station's name start with the same letter as ours?', 'If your nearest Library the same as ours?').

measuring

Seekers ask for if the Hider is closer or further to a specific type of place (e.g. Hospitals) than they are.

Radar

Seekers select a specific distance (e.g., 5km) to project a Radar from their current location. The Hider must answer 'Yes' or 'No' to whether their location falls within that Radar.

thermometer

Seekers travel a specific distance (e.g., 2km) providing the Hider with their starting and end point. The Hider must reveal if the Seekers are now Warmer (closer) or Colder (further) to the Hider's position.

tentacles

Seekers select a category. The webapp mathematically divides the entire map into distinct regions originating from the chosen category. The Hider must reveal which 'tentacle' (region) contains their location.

photo

Seekers request a photograph from the Hider's current perspective. The photo must accurately represent their location without being intentionally misleading. The Hider may edit the photo to blackout words.

5. Time Penalties

To balance the flow of information, the game utilizes a Time Penalty system.

  • Cost of Information: Every time the Seekers ask a question to narrow down the Hider's location, a Time Penalty is incurred.
  • How it Works: This penalty compensates the Hider for revealing clues about their location. Each question type carries a specific time cost that is applied to the game clock.
  • Strategic Seeking: Because asking questions carries a time cost, Seekers must work together to ask the most efficient and strategic questions possible.

(Note: The webapp automatically calculates, tracks, and applies all Time Penalties as questions are logged).

6. Ending the Round

  • The Catch: Seekers must physically locate the Hider. Once a Seeker spots the Hider (or gets within a predetermined visual tagging distance), the round immediately ends.
  • Logging the Time: The webapp calculates the total time the Hider survived, factoring in their baseline hidden time plus any accumulated Time Penalties from the Seekers' questions.
  • Next Round: The players reset, a new Hider is chosen, and the next head start begins.
